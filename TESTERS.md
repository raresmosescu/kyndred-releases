# Kyndred Desktop — Alpha Tester Guide

Thanks for testing! Kyndred puts an animated AI companion on your desktop —
she floats over your apps, you talk to her, she talks back and remembers you.

## Install (2 minutes)

1. **Use your existing Kyndred account** — the same email you use on
   getkyndred.com. (There's no signup inside the app; if you don't have an
   account yet, ask Rares to set you up first.)
2. Download **Kyndred_x64-setup.exe** from the link Rares sent you.
3. Run it. Windows will show a blue **"Windows protected your PC"** screen the
   first time (we're a new unsigned app — a certificate is in progress):
   click **More info → Run anyway**. The install needs no admin rights.
4. Kyndred starts. Enter your account email → check your inbox for a
   **one-time code** → type it in. You stay signed in after that.

## Using her

- She appears as a floating character over your desktop — always on top,
  clicks pass straight through her, so she never blocks your work.
- **Just talk.** The mic is open when she's idle; speak normally and she
  answers out loud. First time, allow microphone access when Windows asks.
- **Type instead:** press **Ctrl+Shift+K** for a small chat box (great when
  you can't talk out loud). Esc closes it.
- **Move / resize her:** press **Ctrl+Shift+E** (edit mode) — drag her
  anywhere, scroll to zoom, press it again to lock her back in.
- **Tray icon (bottom-right):** show/hide her, mute the mic, start a new
  conversation, recenter, quit.
- **Updates install themselves** — if a dialog offers an update, accept it.
  That's how fixes reach you; no re-downloading.

## When something breaks

- A small **status chip** appears top-right with a code like `E-STT-DOWN`.
  **Screenshot it and send it to Rares** — the code tells us exactly what broke.
- For anything weirder, also send the log file:
  `%APPDATA%\com.getkyndred.app\logs\app.log`
  (paste that path into the File Explorer address bar).
- App frozen/lost? Right-click the tray icon → Quit, then start it again from
  the Start menu. Your sign-in and conversation survive restarts.

## What to tell us (the feedback we actually want)

- Did she feel *present* — like she's really there with you — or like a gadget?
- Voice: too slow to answer? Did she ever talk over you or answer herself?
- Did her mouth match her voice?
- What did you *want* to do that she couldn't?
- Would you keep her running all day? Why / why not?

## Honest limitations right now

- English only. Windows only. One character (Maya).
- She can't see your screen yet (coming later).
- Occasional rough edges are the point of an alpha — tell us about all of them.
